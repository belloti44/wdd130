History

Over the years, the Mumias White Water Rafting Company has become a beacon of adventure for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Since its establishment, the company has served a diverse clientele, each leaving with unforgettable memories. Families seeking bonding moments, corporate groups on team-building retreats, and international tourists exploring the wild beauty of Mumias have all found joy in our services. One notable trip involved a group of students from Nairobi who described the experience as “the ultimate adrenaline rush.” Guided through cascading rapids and serene stretches, they marveled at the natural scenery and promised to return. Another memorable expedition catered to a couple celebrating their anniversary. They praised the expert guides for ensuring safety while making the trip thrilling and romantic. Corporate clients have consistently lauded the company for its team-building activities, citing significant improvements in their communication and trust after rafting adventures. Tourists often highlight the personalized service, cultural immersion, and environmental conservation efforts, which make the experience unique. The company’s commitment to excellence and passion for adventure has made it a favorite in Kenya’s outdoor tourism scene. With each trip, clients leave not only satisfied but inspired to embrace life’s wild and beautiful moments.
